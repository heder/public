Privacy Policy - Music Sheet Viewer

Music Sheet Viewer is an app for viewing music sheets in pdf format. The app does not collect or transmit personal information such as names, email addresses, contacts, location, or user-created files.

To help improve app stability and quality, the app may send anonymous telemetry and error reports to an analytics provider (Microsoft Application Insights). This telemetry is strictly limited to technical information such as: error messages, stack traces, app version, device platform and general diagnostic data about the conditions that caused a crash or error.

Telemetry is only used only to diagnose problems and improve the app.

Opting Out

Telemetry and error reporting can be turned off. To opt out, find the "Send diagnostic & crash reports" toggle in the applications about box and switch it off.
